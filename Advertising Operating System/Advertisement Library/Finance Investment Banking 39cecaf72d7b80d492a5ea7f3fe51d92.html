Business Goal

Associate financial growth with the nation's progress while wishing audiences on Independence Day.

Personal financial success contributes to national development.

Communication Direction

Your prosperity grows alongside India's prosperity.

Big Idea

A single illustrated scene combines multiple financial and national symbols into one unified visual narrative.

Visual Thinking System

Primary

Graphic Symbol Composition (NEW)

Definition

A visual system where the message is communicated by assembling multiple symbolic graphic elements into one coherent illustration, with every object contributing one piece of the communication.

Unlike a single metaphor, the meaning emerges from the combined relationship of several symbols.

Structure

Multiple Symbols
        ↓
Each Symbol Carries One Meaning
        ↓
All Symbols Combine
        ↓
Complete Communication

  • Coins → Financial Returns

  • Rupee Coin → Finance
